Guest Information

Wedding Location

All wedding events except the rehearsal dinner will be held on the grounds of the iHotel.

The iHotel is located just south of the University of Illinois campus and Downtown Champaign. View a map.

Directions and Travel

Champaign is easily accessible via car, train, and airplane.

The closest airports are Willard Airport (Champaign), which American flies out of, and Central Illinois Regional Airport (Bloomington), which has a Delta terminal. Champaign is also an easy drive from the airports in Chicago and Indianapolis. The iHotel offers a free shuttle for guests traveling from Willard Airport.

Amtrak travels from Chicago and Carbondale three times daily. It shares a station with Greyhound, and is also a free shuttle spot for iHotel guests.

Directions to the iHotel are available online.

Hotel Information


If you're interested in staying on site (we will be!), we have reserved a block of rooms at the iHotel for the wedding weekend. You can get a discount rate of $139/night by making a reservation through the block either by calling the hotel at 217-819-5000 and mentioning the Lang-Burnette wedding or by booking online.


The block will be open until mid-March 2016.

Breakfast is included from the iCafe in the hotel lobby. The iHotel offers free shuttle service from the local airport, the main train/bus station, and the University of Illinois campus.


Looking for something different? Champaign-Urbana has a diverse range of hotel options! We recommend checking reviews and booking early—spring is busy and some weekends do sell out across both towns.
